Bio

My name is Alexander Frolov, though I often go by Sasha. Alexander and Sasha Frolov are the same person. I'm currently a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland working on cryptography. My advisors are Ian Miers and Gabe Kaptchuk. Previously, I was an engineer on the Cryptography Engineering team at Meta for 2.5 years, and before that I did my masters and bachelors at Cornell University. My hobbies include cycling, swimming, stand-up/sketch comedy and editing Wikipedia.
